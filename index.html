About Me

Zubair Ahmed is young, ambitious and passionate engineer. A true tech saavy person at heart who never stops learning.He maintains some of his project repositories at GitHub and keeps pushing commits on a regular basis. He is a published author of his research work in the International Journal of Engineering Research & Technology (IJERT) and also a contributing senior member at forums such as XDA Developers Forum. He is also known for his leadership skills that he has shown organizing various fests and events. Asserted with considerably excellent event management capabilities, he also protrays great stage presence in the form of an exuberant Emcee. He is also a seeker of adventure who likes trying out new cusinies, visiting new destinations and most of all meeting new people.
